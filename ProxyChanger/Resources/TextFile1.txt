CommandLine Arguments:
===================
/enable  - Enable proxy setting
/disable - Disable proxy setting
===================
Example(s):
	ProxyChanger.exe /disable
	ProxyChanger.exe /enable 192.168.1.1:8080
===================
Example Local Address Bypass:
	ProxyChanger.exe /enable 192.168.1.1:8080 /bypass
